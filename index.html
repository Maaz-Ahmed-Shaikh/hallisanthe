About the Project

Halli-Santhe Digital is an internship project developed to empower local village artisans by providing a digital marketplace platform. The application helps artisans showcase handmade products while enabling urban customers to discover authentic local crafts through a modern mobile experience powered by Flutter, Firebase and GenAI features.
